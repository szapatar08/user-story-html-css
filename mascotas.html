Dogs

Loyal, domesticated mammals descended from wolves, known as "man's best friend" for their companionship, diverse roles (working, assistance), and unique traits like keen senses (smell, hearing) and emotional understanding

Cats

Small, carnivorous mammals known for their agility, sharp senses (sight, hearing, smell), flexible bodies, retractable claws, and diverse fur patterns, serving as popular, independent yet affectionate companions that communicate through meows, purrs, and body language

Mouse

Small rodent known for its pointed snout, large ears, round eyes, slender body, and long, thin tail, often gray or brown
